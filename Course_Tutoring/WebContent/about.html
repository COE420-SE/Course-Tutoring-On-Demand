About Page

This WebSite allows students to register and request for tutoring sessions for any course offered by AUS.

Students can also apply to become tutors and offer tutoring sessions as long as they have a grade of B+ or above in that course. Tutor requests are processed by our Department Admins, the student will be notified when a decision is made.

AUS Faculty Members can use our WebSite to monitor student and tutor activities as long as they have been registered by a Department Admin.
